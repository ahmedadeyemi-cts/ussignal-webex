Audit requirement (summary)

Provider must offer proactive monitoring as part of the cloud managed calling service. CPE devices are monitored for availability and health from the Provider’s operations center (not reactive customer-only reporting). Provider must provide operations procedure documentation or demonstration of the network management system showing end customer device status.

Audit language
US Signal performs proactive monitoring for managed Webex Calling services by continuously collecting operational health signals, triaging alerts within defined response windows, and maintaining evidence in operational records. CPE/Local Gateway availability and customer endpoint status are monitored from the US Signal Operations Center rather than relying solely on customer-initiated tickets.

Severity model & response targets

Recommended operational severity mapping for managed cloud calling service (adjust per contract).

Sev 1
Service outage / PSTN down / E911 risk → response ≤ 15 min
Sev 2
Major degradation / site impact → response ≤ 60 min
Sev 3
Limited impact / single site/user → response ≤ 4 hours
Sev 4
Advisory / informational → response ≤ 1 business day
Escalation matrix (example) ⌄
  • NOC (L1): validate alert, determine scope, open incident, perform first-pass remediation.
  • Collaboration Engineering (L2/L3): calling config, routing, dial plan, device provisioning logic, advanced troubleshooting.
  • Carrier/PSTN: trunk incidents, route failures, porting activation issues, upstream outages.
  • Customer Network: QoS/firewall/NAT/proxy issues impacting RTP; collaborate with customer network owners.
