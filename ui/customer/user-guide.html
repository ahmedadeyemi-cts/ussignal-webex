Executive Portal Documentation

All data within this portal is securely sourced from Cisco Webex Control Hub APIs and Cisco Reports services. This platform acts as a single pane of glass designed to provide operational clarity, automation-driven intelligence, SLA visibility, and proactive monitoring for your Webex Calling environment. US Signal engineers leverage the same telemetry displayed here to accelerate troubleshooting and strategic optimization.
Dashboard Overview +

The Dashboard consolidates tenant health metrics including licensing posture, device health indicators, PSTN availability, active incidents, and SLA status into a unified executive summary. This enables leadership to quickly evaluate operational stability and risk exposure.

License Intelligence & Capacity Management +

Displays allocation, consumption, SKU distribution, overage detection, and risk scoring. Automated deficit modeling identifies potential expansion requirements before service disruption occurs. All license metrics are pulled directly from Cisco Control Hub and normalized for enterprise clarity.
